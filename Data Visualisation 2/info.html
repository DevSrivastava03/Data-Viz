How the Categories Work

1. Productivity & Finance

This category includes time spent on tasks related to work, financial management, and personal productivity. It encompasses activities like checking emails, working on tasks, managing expenses, and budgeting. The goal is to help users track how much of their day is spent on focused, goal-oriented work.

2. Social

The Social category represents time spent on social platforms or interacting with others through communication tools. This can include activities like scrolling through social media, chatting with friends or colleagues, or even video calls. This section aims to show users how much time they dedicate to staying connected with their network.

3. Entertainment

The Entertainment category tracks time spent on activities aimed at relaxation and enjoyment. This includes watching videos, playing games, browsing entertainment websites, or any other leisure-related activity. It helps users reflect on how much time they spend on purely fun and recreational activities.

Understanding these categories can help users gain insights into how their time is spent across different areas of life.
